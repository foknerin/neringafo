Website Incommon

I've created a website for the group I'm a member of and it is called Incommon. We band together from different professional work areas, but we all share one common passion - painting. Making people's day by creating stories with paint and brushes in our hands is the main motivation that moves us forward. In this website we briefly introduce ourselves, tell about our projects and share moments of the overall process.

Visit the website: http://incommon.lt

The work delivered by me includes: needs analysis, creative descriptions of members profile, projects descriptions as well as other content of website, contribution to photography of the website, image optimisation, coding, applying responsiveness, content uploading.

Technologies used: HTML/CSS, Bootstrap, jQuery.
